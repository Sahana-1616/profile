The Hand Gesture Recognition

Hand gesture detection involves using computer vision and machine learning techniques to interpret human hand movements and translate them into commands or inputs. This technology has applications in various fields such as human-computer interaction, sign language recognition, virtual reality, and robotics.

Wound Detox:Leveraging ML For Automated Wound Care

This paper presents "Wound Detox," a machine learning-based application designed to classify wounds into five categories: Abrasion, Bruise, Cut, Laceration, and Stab. The system categorizes these wounds into three severity levels: low (Abrasion, Bruise), medium (Cut, Laceration), and high (Stab).
